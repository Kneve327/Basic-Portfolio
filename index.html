About Me

Kiara was in band for nine years and was involved in her high school band's leadership. After graduating, she went to Seminole State College's leadership retreat. Kiara has a background in guest service, having worked at BURGERFI for a year and a half and Universal Orlando Resort for over two years. She has a passion for travelling, meeting new people, and experiencing new cultures. Kiara is dedicated to her work and goal oriented.
